Memberships

We have 3 different membership types:

  1. Gym & Classes Monthly Membership
  2. Gym Only Monthly Membership
  3. Day Pass

You can purchase a membership by:

  1. Coming into the gym and setting up a membership at the reception desk.
  2. You can purchase a membership by going to the "Memberships" tab of this website and clicking the "join now" button.
  3. By downloading the Glofox app and searching Hero's Gym.

All purchases are final, and no refunds will be given. >

There are no contracts with any of our memberships

All our memberships can be stopped any time and there is no cancellation fee.

Please note that the joining fee will apply if you cancel and rejoin.

To cancel any membership please email info@herosgymtslc.com before the 1st of the month or speak to a member of staff within the gym. Please note once a payment has been taken it is non-refundable.

All memberships can be paid using a card or setting up a direct debit. We do not accept cash payments.

All monthly memberships are billed on the 1st of every month and will continue to do so unless cancelled. When you first join you will be charged a pro-rata rate depending on the day of the month you join plus the joining fee if applicable.

Gym Rules

  1. Please be respectful to all other members and staff at all times in the Gym.
  2. Please strip your weights from any bars and machines you are using when finished with your set.
  3. While selfies and pictures are permitted in the gym please keep the overall phone use to an absolute minimum. remember you are here to train, not to scroll through instagram!
  4. Please allow other members to work in with you when using any of the equipment when asked.
  5. Keep your clothes on while on the gym floor!
  6. Any nonsense or behavior that is deemed disruptive, offensive, or abusive will be dealt with by your immediate removal from the gym!

At Hero's Gym we want to create a friendly, helpful and encouraging environment for all our members! We want people to feel like Hero's and any breach to any above rules on a continuing basis will result in your membership being cancelled with no refunds given!
